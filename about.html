About the Project

What is Infernarium?

INFERNARIUM is an immersive digital experience that explores the boundaries between the mortal realm and the unknown. Born from the ancient traditions of Samhain, this project seeks to recreate the mystical atmosphere of the night when the veil between worlds grows thin.

The Vision

We believe that true horror lies not in what we see, but in what we cannot see. Our vision is to craft an experience that lingers in the mind long after the screen goes dark. Through carefully designed atmospheres, cryptic narratives, and interactive rituals, INFERNARIUM invites you to confront the shadows that dwell within.

The Awakening

On Halloween 2026, the gates will open. Those who have left their trail will be the first to witness what lies beyond. The countdown has begun. The ritual is in motion. There is no turning back.

Join the Covenant

By subscribing to our awakening list, you become part of something greater than yourself. You will receive exclusive transmissions, early access to hidden content, and invitations to participate in secret rituals before the public unveiling. Your soul has been marked. We will find you when the time comes.

The Creators

INFERNARIUM is crafted by a collective of digital occultists, visual artists, and sound designers who share a passion for the macabre and the mysterious. We work in the shadows, guided by ancient symbols and modern technology, to bring forth experiences that transcend the ordinary.
